Katherine Zhang

Katherine Zhang is a UI/UX–oriented designer with a background in music, digital content, and fashion projects. She is currently building hands-on experience in user-centered interface design through independent and collaborative projects, including a consumer-facing fashion app where she leads UI design and contributes to product concept development.

With expertise in social media strategy, visual storytelling, and data-informed content planning, she is particularly interested in digital products that bridge the intersection of lifestyle, fashion, and technology, and in creating intuitive, human-centered user experiences.
