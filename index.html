Katherine Zhang

Katherine Zhang is a UI/UX–oriented designer with a background in music, digital content, and fashion projects. She is currently building hands-on experience in user-centered interface design through independent and collaborative projects, including a consumer-facing fashion app where she leads UI design and contributes to product concept development.

With expertise in social media strategy, visual storytelling, and data-informed content planning, she is particularly interested in digital products that bridge the intersection of lifestyle, fashion, and technology, and in creating intuitive, human-centered user experiences.

I’m curious about how creators can build sustainable careers, how social media shapes identity, and how technology can make self-expression easier and more valuable. I’m currently exploring how product design and storytelling can help creators turn personal style into something shareable and scalable. Someone might follow my work because I’m building a creative app and sharing content that blends fashion, music, and lifestyle. My goal is to create tools and content that feel useful, aesthetic, and empowering.

What I’m Building

I’m building a wardrobe + outfit planning app that helps people organize what they own and style outfits faster.

It’s designed for busy students and young professionals who want to look good without overthinking.

It solves the daily “what should I wear?” problem by turning your wardrobe into an easy outfit system.
